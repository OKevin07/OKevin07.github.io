Silverstein

A band that hails form Burlington, Ontario formed in 2000. Named as a homage to author Shel Silverstein, who they all admired as children. They have released a total of nine studio albums. Lineup had remanined unchanged for eleven years since December 2001, consisting of lead vocalist Shane Told, lead guitarist Neil Boshart, rhythm guitarist Josh Bradford, bassist Billy Hamilton, and drummer Paul Koehler. In September 2012, the nad had announced that Neil Boshart had been fired and would be replaced by Paul Marc Rousseau, who also joined Billy Hamilton on backing vocals. The band achieved moderate success with their second studio album, Discovering the waterfront, which was nominated for a juno award and reached No. 34 on Billboard 200 charts, with the following two albums charting at similar position. They have sold over 1,000,000 albums worldwide. The band left long time record label Victory Records in 2010, and after a period with Hopeless Records are currently signed to Rise Records. They release their 9th studio album, Dead Reflection in July 2017

Band Members

  • Shane Told (Lead Vocalist)
  • Neil Boshart (Lead guitarist until 2012)
  • Paul Marc Rousseau (Lead guitarist 2012-present)
  • Josh Bradford (Rhythm Guitarist)
  • Billy Hamilton (Bassist)
  • Paul Koehler (Drummer)
